About Event

A whatsapp group will be created with all the contestants in which a Wikipedia link will be shared along with a clue for a word. The word that corresponds to the 1st clue will be given in the Wiki-pedia page,participants have to click on the answer and it will lead to another wiki link like wise one should have to crack 4 clues,each clue corresponds to a word . They have to note down initial letter of the each word and type it along with our website URL,it will lead them to a set of aptitude based questions in the website. On submitting the answers an YouTube link will be shown provided . This link will take them to a youtube video which is the final part of the game where they are required to crack a tough question. And one who cracks the answer should submit it through a Google form which will be there in the description.
